About Us

Help is Our Main Goal

Pukaar Society is a Uttar Pradesh Based NGO working in multiple pardigm mainly focused on food , education and women safety

Pukaar Society started as a plan meant for multiple paradigm such as encouraging education, providing financial support and establishing a good society. Along with that we do work for upliftment of women in all spheres and general financial aid that we try to collect for someone in actual need. Fortunately, there were many people like us who joined us in the way and today we are a big family of 125 volunteers all round the country. We are not for profit organisation with our office based at Gorakhpur, Uttar pradesh. We work for better education in the early stage(class 1-3) in Rural villages. We have two Education hut at Shankrapur and Adarsh nagar (Gorakhpur). Eduaction huts give rural kids an opportunity to learn at the pace of other private schools. We teach them syllabus as well as extra curriculum and organise cultural activities. Apart from that we organise awareness camps, medical camps and after class Tutions. All these initiatives are free of cost. We prefer donations of goods that can be used by school going children like stationery, sweaters, painting kits etc. We also have a bar where you can volunteer to organise any celebration event at out schools.
